Mathematical Concepts

1. Sample Space (S)

The total volume of space is represented by a cube of dimensions \(10 \times 10 \times 10\).

$$ Vol(S) = 1000 \text{ units}^3 $$

2. The Event (E)

A collision occurs if a satellite falls inside the debris sphere of radius \(R\).

$$ P(E) = \frac{\text{Vol(Sphere)}}{\text{Vol(Cube)}} $$

3. Monte Carlo Method

We rely on the Law of Large Numbers. By simulating thousands of random launches, the experimental result converges to the theoretical probability.
